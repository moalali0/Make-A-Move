Detailed Services

How we take special care of your move.

House Moving

Moving house is one of life's most stressful events. At Made a Move, we aim to make it the easiest part of your transition. Our team is trained to handle furniture of all sizes, from delicate antiques to heavy wardrobes.

How We Do It

  • Pre-Move Planning: We verify access points and parking to avoid surprises.
  • Furniture Protection: All large items are blanket-wrapped and strapped securely in the van.
  • Floor Protection: We respect your home, using covers for carpets and hardwood floors where necessary.
  • Placement: We don't just dump items; we place them exactly where you want them in your new rooms.

Special Care

  • Fragile Items: Extra padding for glassware, mirrors, and antiques.
  • Difficult Access: Trained to navigate tight staircases, narrow hallways, and challenging entry points.
  • Fully Insured: Goods in transit insurance for complete peace of mind.

Commercial & Office Moves

Time is money. Our commercial moving service is designed to minimise downtime for your business. We offer flexible scheduling, including weekends and evenings, so you can be up and running on Monday morning.

How We Do It

  • Site Survey: We assess your current and new premises to plan the most efficient move.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Moves can be scheduled for evenings or weekends to avoid business disruption.
  • IT Equipment Handling: Computers, servers, and sensitive equipment are carefully transported.
  • Disassembly/Reassembly: We handle office desks, workstations, and shelving units.

Special Care

  • Confidentiality: All team members are vetted and trained in handling sensitive documents.
  • IT Coordination: We can work with your IT team for seamless equipment transition.
  • Commercial Insurance: Full liability cover specifically for business premises and equipment.

Professional Packing

Don't want to live among boxes for weeks? Let our professional packers handle it. We can pack your entire home in a day, using high-quality materials to ensure zero breakages.

Our Process

  • Quality Materials: We use double-walled boxes, bubble wrap, and acid-free paper for crockery.
  • Labeling: Every box is clearly labeled by room and content.
  • Wardrobe Boxes: We bring hanging rails so your clothes go straight from wardrobe to box to wardrobe.
  • Unpacking Service: We can unpack at your new home and remove all packing materials.

Special Care

  • Artwork Protection: Corner guards and blanket wrapping for framed items.
  • Electronics: Original boxes used when available, or custom padding solutions.
  • Valuable Inventory: We document high-value items for your peace of mind.

Secure Storage

Whether you have a gap between exchange and completion or just need to declutter, our storage solutions are secure, dry, and affordable.

How We Do It

  • Collection Service: We pick up items from your property and transport them directly to storage.
  • Inventory System: Every item is logged and photographed for your records.
  • Easy Retrieval: Access your items with just 24 hours notice.

Special Care

  • 24/7 Security: CCTV monitored with alarm systems and secure access controls.
  • Climate Controlled: Consistent temperature and humidity levels protect your belongings.
  • Fully Insured: All stored items are covered by our comprehensive insurance.
  • Flexible Terms: Store for a week, a month, or as long as you need.

Rubbish Disposal

Moving often means clearing out items you no longer need. As a licensed waste carrier, we offer ethical and responsible disposal of furniture, household items, and general waste. We recycle and donate wherever possible.

How We Do It

  • Assessment: We review what needs to be removed and provide a clear, upfront quote.
  • Careful Removal: Our team removes items from any floor, including bulky furniture and appliances.
  • Sorting: Items are sorted into recyclable, donatable, and disposable categories.
  • Responsible Disposal: We work with licensed recycling centres and local charities.
  • Documentation: You receive a Waste Transfer Note proving legal and ethical disposal.

Special Care

  • Sustainability Focus: Usable furniture and appliances are donated to local charities.
  • E-Waste Certified: Electronics are taken to certified e-waste recyclers.
  • No Fly-Tipping: We never illegally dump waste and always provide proof of responsible disposal.
  • Property Protection: Our team handles heavy items safely, protecting your floors and walls during removal.
